Muscle Relaxation
  1. Use your hand to make a tight fist.
  2. Hold it for a few seconds, noticing the tension in your muscles.
  3. Slowly open your hand, noticing how it feels.
  4. Repeat this in different parts of your body, feeling the release of tension.
